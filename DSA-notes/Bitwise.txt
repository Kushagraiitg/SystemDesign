- How to set a bit in the number?
	- digit at n'th position
	- num | (1<<n)
- How to unset a bit
	- num & (~(1<<n))
- Toggling a bit at nth position
	- num ^ (1<<n)
- Check if bit at nth position is set or not
	- (num & (1<<n)) == 1 or 0
- Multiply a number by 2
	- num << 1
- Divide by 2
	- num >> 1
- Compute XOR for 1 to n(direct method)
